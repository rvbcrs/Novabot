# Frequently Asked Questions

## Battery & Charging

### Battery Not Charging

**Cause:** Poor contact between charging port and plug.

**Solution:**

1. The charging plug must be **inserted fully** into the charging port
2. Place the charging station **horizontally** to prevent movement due to gravity
3. If unsuccessful: send ambient temperature, charging station location photo, and mower screen image to support@lfibot.com

### Battery Installation

A video tutorial is available for battery installation. Contact support before attempting any internal mower modifications.

- :material-youtube: [Battery installation tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FfnNZtfv9g8)

## Mowing

### Mowing on Hills

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Maximum slope | 24 degrees (45%) |
| Optimal slope | < 20 degrees |

The mower operates on hills up to 24-degree slope, though results may take longer. Best performance on hills less than 20 degrees.

**Tips for slopes:**

- Reduce mower speed on slopes
- Adjust the mowing angle for smoother performance

### Blade Lifespan

- Replace blades **every 1.5-2 months** for optimal mowing
- Regularly monitor blade condition
- A notification feature for blade replacement is planned

### Out of Boundary

If the mower leaves its mapped boundaries:

1. Check antenna location (provide photos for verification)
2. Remove metal objects nearby
3. Avoid tall trees near the antenna
4. Do NOT place the antenna close to glass

## Storage & Maintenance

### Winter Storage

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Working temperature | 4-40°C |
| Storage temperature | > -30°C |
| Charging below 4°C | Charge indoors |

**Storage procedure:**

1. Charge monthly while keeping the mower horizontal
2. Charge indoors if temperature is below 4°C
3. Switch off via the bottom switch and disconnect the adapter
4. Store in a dry location
5. Keep GNSS antenna connected and protected from water
6. Below -30°C: move all equipment indoors
7. **Do not operate below 4°C**

### Flashing Light and Beeping

This is a **security notification** indicating the blades are enabled and the mower is about to start working.

### Bluetooth Shows as Unpaired

Bluetooth is only active for mapping and app connection. It automatically disables when unused to prevent signal interference. Appearing as "unpaired" in phone settings is **normal behavior**.

## Connectivity

### LoRa Data Transmission Error

A data transmission error indicates a temporary LoRa communication interruption between the mower and base station:

- Usually restores automatically
- Manual recovery via the confirmation button
- Contact support if unresolved within 10 minutes

### Verification Code Not Received

1. Check spam/junk mail folders
2. Wait approximately 1 minute for the email to arrive
3. Consider using Gmail as an alternative email provider

### Login Failed After Password Reset

- Ensure the reset email matches the registration email
- Password **cannot contain spaces, special characters, or punctuation marks**

## Scheduling

### Schedule and Mowing Interruptions

!!! warning
    Do **not** manually start mowing after creating a schedule — it makes the schedule ineffective.

| Interruption Type | Behavior |
|-------------------|----------|
| Self-caused (charging, obstacle avoidance) | Mower resumes from where it left off |
| Manually caused (user stops mower) | Mower restarts the entire lawn |

### Update and Shutdown Notes

- Keep the mower on the charging station during updates — do not cut power manually
- If an update gets stuck: remove mower, reboot, retry
- **Shutdown procedure:** Switch off using the red switch, then remove from charging station

## Accessories

### Wheel Warning — Spare Parts

**Front wheel issues** — needed spare parts:

| Part | Specification |
|------|---------------|
| Front wheel bushing | Outer diameter 16.7mm × Inner diameter 8mm × Thickness 33.5mm |
| Bearing clip spring (steel) | 8.0mm × 0.3mm (M8) |
| Front wheel bearing | Model S608 2RS — Outer 22mm × Inner 8mm × Thickness 7mm |

**Back wheel issues:**

- Apply thread locker (Loctite) to M4×12 screws
- Wait 12 hours before use
- Units that can't be fixed should be returned to a service center
